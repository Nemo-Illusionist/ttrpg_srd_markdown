# Combat

## Making an Attack

When you make an attack, you roll a D20 and add your modifier.

### Melee Attacks

| Attack Type | Ability | Range |
|---|---|---|
| Unarmed Strike | Strength | 5 feet |
| Weapon Attack | Strength | 5 feet |

### Ranged Attacks

You can make a ranged attack only against a target within the weapon's range.
